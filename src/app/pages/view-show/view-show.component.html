Description

In acoustics and audio, a transient is a high amplitude, short-duration sound at the beginning of a waveform that occurs in phenomena such as musical sounds, noises or speech. (Wikipedia)

This radio show is all about appreciating the sharp and well defined transients that drum & bass music has to offer. Beyond technicalities, we strive for the music that can steal your focus and make your head shake while adjusting to different moods in a relatively fast-paced manner.

The musical themes approached are not specific and multiple subgenres are considered, however, the show includes a more energetic style of music focused on the art of DJ-ing, blending and manipulation of sound.
